Went on a good 5k run this morning around the Fens. Lot of shade, lot of robins, lot of good trail. No foot problems at all. I suspect it is excess walking (along with excess running) that still causes tangible discomfort. Not great, but at least better since that means I can still run in the morning as long as I monitor my total steps each day and don't walk too much.

We're at about 5000 steps so far since I ran 5k and I guess each stride is somewhere around a meter plus some walking at the beginning and end. 1482 kanji (!) and page 60 of Sisters in Yellow.

Going to have so many sloppy reviews today since I barely remember the new vocab from yesterday but we're going to just go for 1500 and then, well, keep going. 

I did make it to Billy's Sub Shop yesterday. Waited for the 55 bus and it never came so 30 minute walk instead through the South End. (Probably should have taken the Green Line instead to save some steps). It was everything that I wanted - small family run no fuss sandwich shop with enormous portions. 

I got the pastrami sub since it's been so long since I had one and was thinking about Perly's in RVA. It was good but next time I'm definitely doing the steak bomb or maybe an italian sub or maybe a breakfast sandwich. The cutlets looked good too. What a good spot.
